About Us

The word AHIBO means “to come” or “will come” in Nagamese. Ahibo LLP established in the year 2019. Our sector is the logistics industry. In North East India region, we are the first company as an aggregator whose fleets are only zero-emission vehicles. The direct beneficiaries are the local stores and shops, since we act as a 3rd party logistics provider, which helps them reach out to more customers. We use and encourage only environmentally friendly vehicles by omitting fuel-driven vehicles. Making people aware of the problem of the increase in carbon footprint and getting their product delivered to their door step at a much lesser rate.

With the use of our service, the cost of procuring goods gets minimized as transportation of goods is done at an affordable cost by use of zero emission vehicles like electric vehicle, bicycle etc. This also maximises the profit margin. Our company helps by protecting the environment and also by not affecting the importance of last-mile delivery in today’s digital world.

Our Mission & Vision

We are passionate about establishing a sustainable future by reducing greenhouse gas emissions and supporting renewable energy alternatives at Zero Emission. Our objective is to help future generations live on a cleaner, healthier planet. We seek to deliver new and practical solutions to help businesses, organisations, and individuals move to zero-emission practises using our experience and passion. We aim to reduce our carbon footprint and maximise environmental advantages by combining modern technologies, renewable energy sources, and sustainable practises.
